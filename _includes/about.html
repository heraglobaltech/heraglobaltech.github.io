About


Hera Global Tech Inc. is a woman led company developing products to improve personal safety.
Headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA it was founded in 2017.

The company creates ‘Abel’ and 'Volition.' Both systems intuitively detect high-risk situations based on personal responses and alerts identified contacts that their help is needed.

Abel was a semi-finalists in the 2018 Anu & Naveen Jain Safety XPRIZE competition.

Volition was a 2020 National Science Foundation, SBIR (Small Business Invetigative Research) awardee.

Hera Global Tech supports social organizations and efforts that address universal problems to bring about meaningful impact in the short term and long term.
